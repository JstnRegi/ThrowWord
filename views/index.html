How To Play


Every turn a team will have a guesser. If your guesser says the Throw Word速 press ENTER to get the point. It will then be the other teams turn. The guesser will now quickly rotate with another member of their team, and will be they will be the guesser of their next turn. The guesser can opt to pass in order pause their team's timer. However, the other team has the chance to get the point. In the event both team's guessers pass consecutively, neither team will get the point and the next Throw Word速 will appear on screen.

Rules of the game



Team's can guess as long as their is on their clock. If a team's clock has reached 0, the other team may keep guessing until their clock has run out as well, or simply choose to pass and end the game. Team's describing the Throw Word速 may not say things along the lines of, "It rhymes with..." and "It sounds like...". Teams may charade individually or as one team if they wish. Keep it fair. The winner

The winner of the game is whoever has the most points when both team's clocks reach 0.
